Meet the owner

I'm the owner of Very Nice Studio. I capture the beautiful pictures of people. Born in East Kitchener, grew up in Tokyo, Japan. My first years were spent in struggling. I now live in New York City. I travel to India often. My goal is to show the beauty, grace, strength & enthusiasm of people. I focus on non-models & place regular people in exaggerated situations that highlight an aspect of who they are. I look for a perfection & satisfaction in my work. I'm based in New York City but travel for projects arround the globe. As well as a bit of photo assisting for Annie in New York and Mario in London. I shoot a lot of portraiture as well as assignments for agencies like Sony, Z-Cinema and Marvel.
